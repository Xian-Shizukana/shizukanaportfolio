Personal Growth Plan

Plan to stay motivated and purposeful

In order for me to be motivated, I usually follow my past teacher's lesson about Ikigai. It is essentially 4 things that are needed to have a fulfilling and meaningful life. What I'm reaching for right now should be what I'm good at, what I love, what the world needs, and what I can be paid for. As long as these 4 are met, my life would have a purpose, and I would be motivated to keep doing what I do.

How to Seek Help and Overcome Challenges

In order for one to seek help to overcome a challenge, they must first realize that they're not all-powerful. Usually, it's ego that prevents us from seeking help because we believe we can do it by ourselves. Once we break that ego, once we realize that we are not the only ones in the world, asking for help will become easy.

As for overcoming challenges, you need to be in a right mindset. If you look at it as an insurmountable wall, that will be all that it is. If you look at it as a mountain, then the possibility of reaching the summit becomes accessible to you. One should change their mindset from "Can I?" to "I can."
